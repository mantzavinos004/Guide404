🔥 1. Havoc
Havoc is an open-source post-exploitation Command and Control (C2) framework, similar to Cobalt Strike or Sliver.
It’s used by ethical hackers, red teams, or threat actors to control compromised machines (called agents or demons) during simulated or real attacks.

🛠️ It helps you generate payloads (malicious programs).

📡 Communicates with infected targets over a C2 channel.

🎮 Provides a GUI for managing sessions, sending commands, uploading files, etc.
